Quickstart Guide

This tutorial presumes that you have successfully created an account on Retinad. If you haven't, sign up right now to continue.

You can follow this Video Tutorial, or read the instructions below.

  • 1. Download the SDK

    Click here to download the Plugin.

  • 2. Create your app

    Now, in order to track your analytics, we need to know a bit about your app. If you haven't created an app yet, go in "My Apps" (at the top of the screen) and create a new app by clicking "+". Now, in the app settings you should see 2 keys : “App ID” and “App Secret”. You will need those keys in Unity.

  • 3. Open Unity

    Open Unity and your project’s first scene. Make sure you have imported the VR Headset’s SDK before (I.E: Oculus SDK, Samsung Gear VR SDK, etc). In the menu bar, find “Asset” à “Import Package” à “Custom Package”

  • 4. Find

    Find Retinad's Plugin that you previously downloaded on your computer.

  • 5. Import

    Import all files.

  • 6. Assets

    In the lower left corner, in your Assets, find Retinad's folder. Open it to reveal "Retinad VR Plugin".

  • 7. Scene

    Drag and drop the plugin into your scene.

  • 8. Parameters

    Click the "Retinad VR Plugin" instance in your scene to change its parameters.

  • 9. Sync API Keys

    You will see at the right two fields: "App Key" and "API Key". Told you you would need them! (To find those numbers, go in your dashboard, in your app's page and click the "Show secret keys" button.) Copy and paste the values in Unity.

  • 10. Compile and Run

    You're done! Now compile and run your app. You should be able to already see a change in your metrics dashboard. Congrats, you are your first user!

    You are now ready to package and ship your app. Enjoy your growth!
