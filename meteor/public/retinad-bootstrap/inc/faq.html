FAQ for developers

  • What service are you offering?

    Retinad offers a complete and easy to use analytics solution for Virtual Reality developers. We allow developers to track powerful metrics about their app, for free.

  • Is this product for me?

    Are you developing apps for VR ? You got your answer! Please note that we only support Unity at the moment. Not using Unity ? Tell us which IDE you are using at support@retinadvr.com

  • Is this useful?

    After putting so much effort in developing the perfect experience for your users, wouldn't you want to know their appreciation of your work ? Let the numbers speak for themselves. Track metrics like the number of users, engagement metrics like daily, weekly or monthly active users, average usage duration, and much more. Start tracking all this in 5 minutes, and for free!

  • How much is it?

    Retinad Analytics is completely free. No limit, no catch, just plain free. What’s not to love.

  • I signed up. Now what?

    Congratulations, you’re halfway done ! Follow the steps in our quickstart guide and start tracking powerful metrics with no single line of code.

  • Who can access my data? Is it public?

    Your data is completely private and confidential. No one but you has access to it. We use the safest security standards available to ensure your protection.

  • I have a question. Where can I get an answer?

    It will be our pleasure to assist you. Email us at support@retinadvr.com. If you have any suggestion or comment about the product, we’d love to hear your opinion! feedback@retinadvr.com

  • What are you compatible with?

    We support Oculus Rift, Samsung Gear VR and Google Cardboard. Other devices and platforms will be supported soon! If you are targeting a specific platform or device that is not supported, please contact us at support@retinadvr.com

  • What are sessions? How are they counted?

    A new session is created every time a user opens your app and interacts with it. The session ends when the user is done using the app. So for example, if your neighbor uses your app twice a day, he will have contributed to 2 new sessions every day.
